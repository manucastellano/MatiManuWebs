Welcome to TNM Tutoring

We are a team of highly experienced and qualified tutors with nearly a decade of teaching experience in several distinguished academic institutions. Our passion for education has led us to establish our own tutoring company, where we aim to reach a wider range of students with our expert services.

Our innovative pricing model is designed to make high-quality educational services accessible and affordable to everyone, with rates that are 80% lower than the industry average. With our commitment to excellence, we are dedicated to helping our students achieve their academic goals and reach their full potential.

About Us

Who we are

We employ a personalized approach for each student, meticulously tailoring their teaching methods to suit your individual needs. This includes aligning your learning goals and strategies with the topics that you cover, ensuring that your tutor's teaching style suits your learning style, and providing the necessary support for you to excel in your academic pursuits.

Our tutors are carefully hired and trained to be experts not just on the test but on building learning plans to fit each student. When you meet with one of our tutors, you won't be listening to them read out of a book, and you won't be sitting through some pre-set curriculum: you will be actively learning and working with your tutor to build the knowledge and strategies that work for you. We like to create a positive and supportive learning environment where students feel comfortable asking questions and taking risks. Overall, our goal is to empower students to achieve their goals and reach their full potential.

Our Rates

Payment Methods:

We offer several convenient payment methods, including wire transfer, Zelle, and other online banking services.

Referral Program:

We appreciate our students' loyalty and support, and that's why we have a referral program that rewards them for spreading the word about our services. For every new student referred, the referring student will receive a 50% discount on their next lesson.

Discount Packages:

At our academy, we offer a variety of pricing options to suit your needs and budget. For those who want to make a longer-term commitment, we offer a discount for purchasing a certain number of hours upfront. If you purchase more than 10 hours, you'll receive a 10% discount, and if you purchase more than 20 hours, you'll receive a 15% discount.

We also understand that some students prefer to pay on a week-to-week basis, so we offer affordable rates for individual lessons as well. Our innovative pricing model is designed to make high-quality educational services accessible and affordable to everyone, so you can feel confident that you're getting the best value for your investment.

At TNM, we believe that having access to quality study materials is essential for academic success. That's why we've created our own comprehensive library of study materials, featuring our own test prep guides and material, hundreds of practice tests, study books, and other resources, all designed to help our students achieve their full potential. We know that preparing for exams can be a daunting task, which is why we're committed to providing our students with everything they need to succeed. And the best part? All of our test prep materials are completely free for our students to use, so they can focus on their studies without worrying about the added expense of purchasing study materials.

How does it work?

Through the use of overhead cameras, screenshare, and whiteboard technology, your tutor will visually present diagrams, examples, and work out problems with you, while being specially trained to ensure effective online teaching. In fact, our students often tell us they tend to forget that they are meeting their tutors online, as the experience feels so natural that it is like being in the same room.

Our SAT and ACT preparation books are the result of years of research and expertise from our team of experienced educators. We understand that these tests can be daunting, which is why we've designed our books to be the most comprehensive and effective resources available.

Our books are organized into chapters that cover all the topics involved in the SAT and ACT exams, ensuring that our students have a deep understanding of the concepts they need to know for test day. Each chapter includes strategies and practice questions to help students master the material.

Unlike other test prep books that only provide a surface-level understanding of the exams, our books go into depth on the most important concepts and skills needed for success. Our students have consistently achieved impressive results thanks to our books, and we're confident that you'll see the same success when you choose to use them for your own test prep.

Whether you're just starting to study for the SAT or ACT, or you're looking to improve your score, our books are the ultimate resource for achieving your goals.
